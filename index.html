I
Chapter One

The Jamas

We boarded the bus as boys in 1990. We stepped off as men in 1996. What was forged in those six years has never broken.

Ngo Bus '90 — the Jamas — is the collective of those who walked those Namilyango halls together. The boarding houses, the chapel mornings, the rugby pitches, the dining halls.

Thirty years on, we remain bound by something neither time nor distance has unstitched.
