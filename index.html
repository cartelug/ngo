I
Chapter One

The Jamas

We boarded the bus as boys in 1990. We stepped off as men in 1996. What was forged in those six years has never broken.

Ngo Bus '90 — the Jamas — is the collective of those who walked those Namilyango halls together. The boarding houses, the chapel mornings, the rugby pitches, the dining halls, the prefect appointments, the leavers' prom at Nabisunsa.

Thirty years on, we remain bound by something neither time nor distance has unstitched. This is our digital home — for memory, for the living, and for those who have gone ahead of us.

NICE
Ngo Bus Investment Club Endeavour

Not every brother is in N.I.C.E. — and that is by design. But for those who choose to pool their capital, their judgment, and their patience, this is how Ngo Bus '90 builds wealth, together.

Reports are published. Projects are visible in real time. The committee communicates openly. You can see exactly what N.I.C.E. is up to right here.

Our Vision

"To compound the trust of the brotherhood into generational wealth — through disciplined investment, transparent reporting, and shared upside. What we build together, we build for our children."
